Digital Circuit Design - Set 1

1. 1. What are the two operating regions of a MOSFET when used as a switch?

Cut-off (OFF) and Saturation (ON) regions.

2. Differentiate between continuous-time and discrete-time signals.

Continuous-time: Defined for all t, example: AC mains

Discrete-time: Defined only at discrete instants, example: ADC output

3. What are the main classifications of signals?

Deterministic / Random

Periodic / Aperiodic

Even / Odd

Energy / Power

Analog / Digital

4. What is an even signal? Give an example.

An even signal satisfies x(t) = x(-t).

Example: x(t) = t².

5. Explain energy and power signals.

Energy signals have finite energy.

Power signals have finite power over infinite duration.
