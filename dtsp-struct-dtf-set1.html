Group delay represents how much delay different frequency components of a signal experience when passing through the filter. It measures the phase distortion introduced by the filter.

Importance: If group delay varies significantly with frequency, the filter distorts the shape of signals (e.g., speech waveforms). Hence, group delay analysis is crucial in IIR systems to check how much dispersion (signal spreading) occurs.
